Welcome to Mentor Hub
Your new home for managing mentorship sessions, tracking tasks, and staying connected with your team. Everything you need in one place.

Dashboard Overview
See your upcoming sessions, pending tasks, and recent activity at a glance with role-specific dashboards for students, mentors, and staff.

Session Management
Schedule, view, and manage mentorship sessions with your team. See session details, agendas, and participant information all in one place.

Task Tracking
Create and track action items from your sessions. Update status, set priorities, and mark tasks complete as you make progress.

Team Collaboration
View your team members, see who's assigned to each session, and keep everyone aligned on goals and progress.

Feedback Collection
Submit and review feedback after sessions to track progress and identify areas for improvement.

Session Deletion for Staff
Staff members can now remove sessions that were created in error or are no longer needed, with a confirmation step to prevent accidents.
