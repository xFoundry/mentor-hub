title: Pre-Meeting Prep & Team Pages
date: 2025-12-04
---

Pre-Meeting Submissions
Submit agenda items and questions before your sessions so mentors can prepare and your time together is more productive.

In-Person Session Locations
Sessions can now include a physical location for in-person meetings, displayed clearly on the session details page.

Improved Team Pages for Students
Students now have a tabbed interface on team pages, making it easier to navigate between overview, sessions, tasks, members, and feedback.

Smarter Session Creation
When creating a new session, the date and time now default to the current date with time rounded to the next hour, saving you a few clicks.
