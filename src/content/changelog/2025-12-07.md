date: 2025-12-07

Meeting Prep Reminders
Receive email reminders before your sessions with helpful prompts to submit any pre-meeting questions or agenda items.

Feedback Reminders
Get gentle nudges to submit feedback after sessions, helping keep the feedback loop active and useful for everyone.

Eastern Time Display
All session times now display consistently in Eastern Time throughout the portal and in email notifications, so there's no confusion about when meetings start.

Session Update Notifications
When session details change (time, location, or meeting link), participants are notified so everyone stays on the same page.
